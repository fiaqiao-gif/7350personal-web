Popular Cat Breeds

Ragdoll

Origin: United States

Known as the "gentle giants" of the cat world, Ragdolls are extremely affectionate and docile. They often go limp when picked up, hence their name.

Appearance: Long, silky fur, blue eyes, large size.
Personality: Calm, social, loves human companionship.
Care: Weekly brushing to prevent matting.

British Shorthair

Origin: United Kingdom

One of the oldest cat breeds, the British Shorthair is known for its round face, dense coat, and easygoing nature—perfect for apartment living.

Appearance: Stocky build, dense short fur, copper/gold eyes.
Personality: Independent, calm, low-maintenance.
Care: Bi-weekly brushing, monitor weight to avoid obesity.

American Shorthair

Origin: United States

A sturdy and healthy breed, the American Shorthair is playful yet gentle, making them great family pets. They have a distinctive tabby pattern.

Appearance: Muscular build, short thick fur, variety of patterns.
Personality: Playful, adaptable, good with children.
Care: Weekly brushing, regular exercise to stay active.

Siamese

Origin: Thailand (Siam)

Famous for their vocal nature and striking color-point coat, Siamese cats are highly intelligent and form strong bonds with their owners.

Appearance: Slender body, short fur, blue eyes, color points on ears/face/tail.
Personality: Vocal, social, intelligent, demands attention.
Care: Minimal brushing, lots of interactive play.

Exotic Shorthair

Origin: United States

Known as the "lazy man's Persian", they have the Persian's cute flat face but short fur. They are quiet and affectionate lap cats.

Appearance: Round face, short dense fur, compact body.
Personality: Quiet, laid-back, loves to cuddle.
Care: Weekly brushing, clean facial folds regularly.

Maine Coon

Origin: United States (Maine)

The largest domestic cat breed, Maine Coons are gentle, playful, and known for their bushy tails and water-resistant fur.

Appearance: Giant size, long shaggy fur, bushy tail, tufted ears.
Personality: Gentle, playful, good with other pets.
Care: Weekly brushing, regular nail trimming (due to size).
